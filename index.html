Trazo Studio

Trazo is a video game studio based in Chile. Our aim is to create original narratives for niche audiences.

Alhué

The game introduce us to "Alhué", a young boy arrived as a tourist to "Isla Grande de Chiloé" (Chile). As soon as he steps on the island, the landscape transforms.

Warlock

A man with a black cloak and hat descends, who before falling into the sea is received by a figure similar to a horse made of water. Before this individual mounted on the strange being reached the pier, a female figure covered in a dress of seaweed emerges from the sea foam. This figure shoots something like tentacles of seaweed from its fingers, managing to wrap the mysterious horseman in black and throw it into the water.

A son

Alhué faints and wakes up after a while very confused, believing that it was all a dream.
